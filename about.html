About
Us

Buds & Grabba: The Infused Experience was born from the heart of the culture — where music, food, and smoke intertwine in moments that linger long after the night ends. Founded by Chelle and Kay, two visionary duo with deep roots in the Caribbean and urban creative scenes, Buds & Grabba is more than a brand — it’s a movement, a memory, and a lifestyle.

What started as a simple idea then a logo quickly grew into something greater: a curated experience infused with flavor, energy, and authenticity. From intimate gatherings to large-scale events, we’ve built a platform that brings people together to celebrate life, elevate vibes, and support community — all while staying grounded in who we are and where we come from.

At Buds & Grabba, we blend the richness of Caribbean influence with premium cannabis culture, crafting unforgettable infused experiences that engage all the senses. Whether it’s a curated session, a signature pop-up, or a private brunch, we specialize in transforming ordinary moments into luxury-infused memories.

Chelle leads with bold creative energy and an unwavering love for the culture. Kay brings culinary precision and a refined sense of style. Together, they’ve built a brand that lives at the intersection of taste, expression, and elevated living.

Buds & Grabba is for the ones who know — that the real luxury is in the vibe, the connection, and the experience.
